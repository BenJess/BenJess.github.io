Grid

Guiding Principles

Our grid system is made up of a flexible column style based on bootsrap4. 1 col spans, 2 col spans, 3 col spans, 4 col spans, and a 6 col span(Rarely used). For the majority of our use cases you will be using a 1 col, or 2 col span with a device mandated min-width.
