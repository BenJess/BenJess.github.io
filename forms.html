Forms

Guiding Principles

Most of our forms, such as text inputs, text areas, and form validations are fairly basic. There are a few exceptions to this rule, in the form of custom drop-downs, checkboxes, radio buttons, and search fields. Form fields are flex box responsive. Required fields will contain a * required next to the field name - for WCAG compliance, and if the user attempts to progress to the next step without entering any field information the box will hightlight red *See "Input with error" below.
